Included pipes in Angular - Usually in the Common Module

Original: {{totalSells}}
Pipe: totalSells | number:'1.2-2'
Result: {{totalSells | number:'1.6-6'}}

Original: {{totalSells}}
Pipe: totalSells | currency:'USD':'symbol-narrow':'1.4-4'
Result: {{totalSells | currency:'USD':'symbol-narrow':'1.4-4'}}

Original: {{percent}}
Pipe: totalSells | percent:'1.2-2'
Result: {{percent | percent:'1.2-2'}}
